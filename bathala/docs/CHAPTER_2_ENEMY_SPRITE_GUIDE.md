# Chapter 2 Enemy Sprite Guide

This file lists the Chapter 2 enemies that need dedicated art direction and AI-ready prompts.

Chapter theme: `The Submerged Barangays`
Visual direction: flooded ruins, moonlit coasts, drowned villages, sea spirits, water-fire tension, simple readable pixel art.

All prompts below follow these rules:
- `1:1 aspect ratio`
- `simple pixel art`
- `same art style as the game Bathala`
- `not overly detailed`
- `clean readable silhouette`
- `limited color palette`
- `no text`
- `no UI`

Each enemy includes 2 prompt types:
- `Splash art` = more dramatic character-focused illustration
- `Simple bg` = same character but with a light, simple background suitable for game asset generation

## Enemies Needing Sprites

### 1. Sirena Illusionist
- Needs sprite: `Yes`
- Tier: `Common`
- What the image should show:
  A dangerous but elegant sirena using song and illusion, more mystical and manipulative than mournful.
- Key visual elements:
  - Mermaid-like sea spirit
  - Long flowing hair
  - Enchanting pose or singing posture
  - Illusion or magical water glow
  - Distinctly a sirena, not a generic mermaid
- Simple art brief:
  `A sea enchantress who lures victims with beauty and song, mixing grace with danger.`
- Splash art prompt:
  `Create a 1:1 simple pixel art splash art sprite in the same art style as the game Bathala. Do not make it overly detailed. Keep it clean, readable, and visually strong with simple shapes and a limited color palette. Show Sirena Illusionist as an elegant but dangerous Filipino sea spirit with long flowing hair, a graceful fish tail, and an enchanting singing pose. Add subtle illusion magic or glowing water around her to show that she manipulates minds and song. Make her look like a sirena guardian corrupted into a lure, not a generic fantasy mermaid. No text, no UI.`
- Simple bg prompt:
  `Create a 1:1 simple pixel art enemy sprite in the same art style as the game Bathala with a simple background. Do not make it overly detailed. Show Sirena Illusionist as a readable full-body sea spirit with long hair, fish tail, and a magical singing posture, set against a simple coastal or shallow-water background with minimal rocks or waves. Keep the background light and uncluttered so the character remains the focus. No text, no UI.`
