# Chapter 2 Relic Sprite Guide

This file lists the Chapter 2 relics that need dedicated relic sprite art prompts.

Chapter theme: `The Submerged Barangays`
Visual direction: sea relics, sacred ocean objects, drowned myth, water-fire tension, simple readable icon art.

All prompts below follow these rules:
- `1:1 aspect ratio`
- `pixelated art style`
- `same art style as the game Bathala`
- `simple readable relic sprite`
- `not overly detailed`
- `clean silhouette`
- `limited color palette`
- `no text`
- `no UI`
- `plain white background only`

## Relics Needing Sprites

### 1. Sirena's Scale
- Needs sprite: `Yes`
- Type: `Common`
- What the image should show:
  A shimmering scale from a sirena, elegant and magical rather than ordinary fish armor.
- Key visual elements:
  - Single large sea scale
  - Iridescent or luminous feel
  - Graceful sea-spirit identity
- Simple art brief:
  `A beautiful magical scale from a sirena, sacred and oceanic.`
- AI prompt:
  `Create a 1:1 simple relic sprite in pixelated art style in the same art style as the game Bathala. Show Sirena's Scale as a single elegant magical sea scale with a shimmering ocean-spirit look. Make it clearly a sirena relic and not a generic fish scale. Keep it simple, readable, and not overly detailed, with a clean silhouette and limited color palette. Use a plain white background only. No text, no UI.`
