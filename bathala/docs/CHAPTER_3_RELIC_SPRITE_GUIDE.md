# Chapter 3 Relic Sprite Guide

This file lists the Chapter 3 relics that need dedicated relic sprite art prompts.

Chapter theme: `The Skyward Citadel`
Visual direction: celestial omens, sacred birds, divine objects, storm relics, sky myths, simple readable icon art.

All prompts below follow these rules:
- `1:1 aspect ratio`
- `pixelated art style`
- `same art style as the game Bathala`
- `simple readable relic sprite`
- `not overly detailed`
- `clean silhouette`
- `limited color palette`
- `no text`
- `no UI`
- `plain white background only`

## Relics Needing Sprites

### 1. Tigmamanukan Feather
- Needs sprite: `Yes`
- Type: `Common`
- What the image should show:
  A prophetic feather from Bathala's omen bird.
- Key visual elements:
  - Sacred feather shape
  - Celestial omen identity
  - Elegant but readable silhouette
- Color palette:
  - Soft sky blue
  - Pale gold
  - Cream white
- Simple art brief:
  `A divine feather from a bird that carries omens in its flight.`
- AI prompt:
  `Create a 1:1 simple relic sprite in pixelated art style in the same art style as the game Bathala. Show Tigmamanukan Feather as a sacred prophetic feather from Bathala's omen bird. Make it clearly a divine sign relic and not a generic feather. Use a distinct color palette of soft sky blue, pale gold, and cream white so it stands apart from the other Chapter 3 relics. Keep it simple, readable, and not overly detailed, with a clean silhouette and limited color palette. Use a plain white background only. No text, no UI.`
