# Requirements Document

## Introduction

The Educational Events System enhances the existing game events to provide cultural education and values formation through Filipino mythology and folklore. This system transforms simple narrative encounters into meaningful educational experiences that teach players about Filipino cultural heritage, moral values, and traditional wisdom while maintaining engaging gameplay mechanics.

## Glossary

- **Educational_Event_System**: The enhanced event system that incorporates educational content, cultural references, and values formation
- **Values_Education**: The process of teaching moral principles, cultural wisdom, and ethical behavior through interactive storytelling
- **Cultural_Reference**: Academic citations and sources for each mythological element or folklore tale used in events
- **Mini_Game_Mechanic**: Interactive gameplay elements within events that go beyond simple choice selection
- **Alamat**: Traditional Filipino legends that explain natural phenomena or cultural practices
- **Folklore_Tale**: Traditional stories passed down through generations that contain moral lessons
- **Academic_Source**: Scholarly references from Filipino mythology experts like Maximo D. Ramos and other folklore researchers

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a player, I want to encounter educational events that teach me about Filipino mythology and culture, so that I can learn about cultural heritage while playing the game.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a player encounters an event THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL display cultural context and educational information about the mythological creature or folklore element
2. WHEN an event is presented THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL include at least one academic reference from Filipino mythology scholars
3. WHEN an event concludes THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL provide a brief explanation of the cultural significance or moral lesson
4. WHERE educational content is displayed THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL maintain engaging narrative flow without overwhelming the player
5. WHEN events reference specific alamats or legends THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL cite the original source or regional variation

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a thesis researcher, I want each event to include proper academic citations and references, so that the educational content has scholarly credibility and can be verified.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an event is created THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL include at least one academic reference from recognized Filipino folklore scholars
2. WHEN referencing Maximo D. Ramos works THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL cite the specific book title and relevant page or chapter
3. WHEN using regional folklore variations THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL note the geographic origin and cultural context
4. WHEN presenting moral lessons THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL reference traditional Filipino values and their cultural importance
5. WHEN displaying references THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL format citations in a consistent academic style

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a player, I want events to include interactive mini-game mechanics beyond simple choices, so that I can have more engaging and memorable educational experiences.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN certain events are triggered THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL present mini-game mechanics that relate to the cultural lesson
2. WHEN mini-games are presented THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL ensure they reinforce the educational content rather than distract from it
3. WHEN players complete mini-game challenges THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL provide rewards that reflect the cultural significance of the achievement
4. WHERE mini-games involve traditional Filipino games or practices THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL explain the cultural context and rules
5. WHEN mini-games conclude THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL connect the gameplay experience to the broader moral or cultural lesson

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a player, I want to encounter at least 5 different educational events per chapter, so that I have varied and comprehensive exposure to Filipino cultural heritage throughout my journey.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a chapter is generated THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL provide at least 5 unique educational events for that chapter
2. WHEN events are selected THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL ensure variety in mythological creatures, moral themes, and cultural regions represented
3. WHEN multiple events are encountered THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL avoid repetition of the same educational content or references
4. WHERE events span multiple chapters THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL progress from simpler to more complex cultural concepts
5. WHEN events are randomized THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL maintain educational coherence and appropriate difficulty progression

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As an educator, I want events to focus on values education and moral lessons, so that players develop understanding of Filipino ethical principles and cultural wisdom.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN events present moral dilemmas THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL reflect traditional Filipino values such as kapamilya, pakikipagkunware, and utang na loob
2. WHEN players make choices THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL provide feedback that explains the moral implications of their decisions
3. WHEN cultural practices are depicted THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL explain their significance in Filipino society and value system
4. WHERE events involve community interactions THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL emphasize Filipino concepts of bayanihan and collective responsibility
5. WHEN moral lessons are presented THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL connect them to contemporary relevance and application

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a player, I want events to include diverse regional folklore from across the Philippines, so that I can learn about the rich cultural diversity of different Filipino communities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN events are created THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL include folklore from major Filipino regions including Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao
2. WHEN regional variations exist THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL acknowledge different versions of the same myth or legend
3. WHEN presenting regional content THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL provide cultural context about the specific community or ethnic group
4. WHERE indigenous traditions are featured THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL treat them with respect and cultural sensitivity
5. WHEN regional diversity is displayed THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL promote understanding of Filipino cultural unity within diversity

### Requirement 7

**User Story:** As a player, I want the educational content to be seamlessly integrated with gameplay mechanics, so that learning feels natural and enjoyable rather than forced or academic.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN educational content is presented THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL maintain the game's narrative tone and pacing
2. WHEN cultural information is displayed THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL use storytelling techniques rather than lecture-style exposition
3. WHEN players interact with educational elements THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL provide immediate gameplay relevance and consequences
4. WHERE academic references are shown THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL present them in an accessible, non-intrusive manner
5. WHEN events conclude THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL ensure players feel entertained and educated simultaneously

### Requirement 8

**User Story:** As a game developer, I want the enhanced event system to be extensible and maintainable, so that new educational content can be easily added and existing content can be updated.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN new events are added THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL support modular event creation with standardized educational components
2. WHEN academic references are updated THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL allow easy modification without affecting gameplay mechanics
3. WHEN cultural content needs revision THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL provide clear separation between educational data and game logic
4. WHERE events require localization THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL support multiple language versions while preserving cultural authenticity
5. WHEN system maintenance is performed THEN the Educational_Event_System SHALL maintain backward compatibility with existing save games and progress
